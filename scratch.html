France Lyon Mission

I served in the France Lyon Mission from 2021-2023, which encompasses the south of France and French Switzerland. While there, I had the privilege of living among the people, falling in love with their language and culture, and growing spiritually by strengthening my testimony of the Savior, Jesus Christ. My mission will forever be some of the most cherished moments of my life, and I am eternally grateful for the lessons learned, experiences had, and the people that I met. I wouldn't trade those two years for anything, and I feel extremely blessed to have been given the opportunity to serve my Savior!

10 Biggest Lessons

  • Jesus Christ has the power to save, heal, and comfort - no matter how deep our pains or trials may feel.
  • All things are made possible through Jesus Christ when there is faith on His name.
  • Heavenly Father hears our prayers, knows us by name, is aware of our needs, and he truly does love us genuinely.
  • You are granted greater access to the hearts of the people as you strive to master every aspect of thier language.
  • Happiness is a choice, and life is so much better when you choose to be happy.
  • The more fully one lives the gospel, the more opportunites there will be to share it.
  • "The greatest discovery of our time is that people can change."
  • Doing is better than knowing.
  • The spirit is the ultimate teacher, and the story of the restoration is the ultimate conduit of the spirit - no matter the language.
  • We recieve more faith by doing something that requires more faith.
